  • Account Setup: Create and verify your SecureAuth account in minutes with email verification
  • Two-Factor Authentication: Add an extra layer of security using authenticator apps or SMS codes

  • Endpoints Reference: POST /auth/login, POST /auth/logout, GET /auth/status, POST /auth/verify

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long do sessions last? Default 30 days or based on your custom settings in Account Settings
  • Can I use multiple devices? Yes! Unlimited devices with individual session management
  • How are passwords encrypted? AES-256 encryption at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit, never stored in plain text
  • What if I forget my password? Use "Forgot Password" to reset via email or authenticated recovery codes
  • Is there offline access? Session key can be used offline for limited operations

🛡️ Security & Privacy Tips

  • ✅ Use a unique, strong password (12+ characters with mixed case, numbers, symbols)
  • ✅ Enable 2FA immediately - the most important security step you can take
  • ✅ Review Sessions regularly and logout unused devices
  • ✅ Check Auth Logs weekly for suspicious activity
  • ✅ Enable login notifications to get alerts for new access
  • ✅ Use hardware security keys (YubiKey, Google Titan) for maximum protection
  • ✅ Store recovery codes in a secure location (password manager, safe deposit box)
  • 🚫 Never share your password or recovery codes with anyone
  • 🚫 Don't use information from your public profile in passwords
  • 🚫 Be careful with links in emails - always visit secureauth.com directly for sensitive actions
